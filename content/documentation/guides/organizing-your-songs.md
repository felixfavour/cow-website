# Organizing Your Songs

As your library grows, finding the right song quickly becomes really important — especially on a Saturday night when you're still finalizing Sunday's setlist!

Here are some simple ways to keep your songs organized. 🗂️

## Use Tags to Group Similar Songs

Tags are like sticky notes you attach to a song. You can add multiple tags to any song, and then filter your library by tag when you need to find something fast.

**Good tag ideas:**
- By genre: `contemporary`, `hymn`, `gospel`, `christmas`
- By mood: `upbeat`, `reflective`, `worship`, `celebration`
- By key: `key-c`, `key-g`, `key-e`
- By season: `advent`, `easter`, `thanksgiving`

### How to add tags:
1. Open any song in your library
2. Look for the **Tags** field
3. Type a tag and hit **Enter** (you can add as many as you like)
4. Click **Save**

> **Pro tip:** Be consistent with your tags. If you use "christmas" for some songs and "Christmas" for others, they'll show up separately when you filter. Lowercase works best!

## Create Folders (Playlists)

Folders let you save groups of songs for quick access. Think of them like playlists on Spotify, but for your worship team.

**Examples of useful folders:**
- "Our Most-Used Songs"
- "Slow Worship Songs"
- "Advent 2025"
- "Songs We're Learning"

### How to create a folder:
1. Go to your **Songs** library
2. Click **+ New Folder** (or "New Playlist" depending on your version)
3. Give it a name
4. Add songs to it by opening a song and clicking **Add to Folder**

## Use the Search Bar

Your song library has a powerful search. You can search by:
- Song title
- Artist name
- Tags
- Lyrics (if you've added them)

Just start typing in the search bar and results appear instantly.

## Sort and Filter

In your song library, you can sort songs by:
- **Recently added** — find your newest songs
- **Alphabetical** — classic and easy
- **Most used** — great for finding your go-to songs
- **Key** — helpful when planning around a specific key for the day

## Keep Songs Updated

When your team figures out a better arrangement — a key change, a new intro, a capo position — update the song in Cloud of Worship so everyone has the latest version.

> **Heads up:** Only Admins and Worship Leaders can edit songs. If you're a Musician and need to suggest a change, use the **Comments** section on the song.
