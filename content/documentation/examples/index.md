# How Real Teams Use Cloud of Worship

## Setting Up Your Weekly Service

Follow along as we help you plan Sunday's service from start to finish.

:::step-by-step
1. **Plan your service** — Choose your 5-6 songs (opening, worship, transition, prayer, closing)
2. **Assign roles** — Mark who leads each song, who plays keys, etc.
3. **Share with your team** — Send the service link so everyone can prepare
4. **Make adjustments** — Update in real-time if songs change Friday night
5. **Go lead worship!** — Your team is prepared and confident
:::

## Organizing 500+ Songs

If your library is growing, here's how to stay organized:

- **Use folders** — Organize by theme, key, or season
- **Tag everything** — Use tags like #contemporary, #hymn, #advent
- **Rate your favorites** — Mark songs you use often with a star
- **Keep notes** — Add "We love this!" or "Good for Christmas" to songs

:::tip
**Need help organizing fast?** You can bulk-import your songs from a spreadsheet. Check out our [import guide].
:::
