## Getting Started

### Do I need to install anything?

Nope! Cloud of Worship runs entirely in your web browser. No downloads, no installations. Just open the website and log in.

### Is it free?

We offer a free plan with core features for smaller teams. For larger teams or advanced features, check out our [pricing page](/pricing) for details.

### Can I use it on my phone?

Yes! Cloud of Worship is designed to work on phones, tablets, and desktops. Many worship leaders use it on their phone during service.

## Songs & Library

### How do I add songs from another app?

You can import songs by copy-pasting lyrics directly into the song editor, or use our bulk import feature (available on some plans). Go to **Songs > Import** to get started.

### Can I add chords to my songs?

Yes! Open a song and click the **Chords** tab. You can add chord notations above the lyrics line by line.

### What if I don't know the key of a song?

No problem — the key field is optional. You can leave it blank and fill it in later. Or just ask your pianist or guitarist — they'll know! 😄

### Can I delete a song?

Yes, but be careful — deleting a song removes it permanently from your library. It will also be removed from any past services it was added to.

> **Tip:** Instead of deleting, try adding a tag like "retired" or moving it to an "Archive" folder. That way you keep a history without cluttering your active library.

## Team & Permissions

### How do I remove a team member?

Go to **Settings > Team**, find the person, click the **⋮** menu next to their name, and select **Remove**.

### Can I change someone's role?

Yes! Go to **Settings > Team**, click the **⋮** menu next to their name, and select **Change Role**.

### What's the difference between roles?

| Role | Can Edit Songs | Can Manage Team | Can See Everything |
|------|---------------|----------------|-------------------|
| Admin | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Worship Leader | ✅ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Musician | ❌ | ❌ | Assigned only |
| Viewer | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |

## Services & Planning

### How far in advance can I plan services?

As far ahead as you'd like! Many teams plan services weeks or even months in advance, especially around big seasons like Christmas and Easter.

### Can my team see the service plan?

Yes — when you publish or share a service, everyone on your team can see the setlist, their assigned songs, and any notes you've added.

### Can I reuse a service from a previous week?

Yes! Open any past service and click **Duplicate**. A copy is created that you can edit for the new date.

## Technical

### Which browsers work best?

Cloud of Worship works best on **Chrome** and **Firefox**. It also works on Safari (for iPhone and Mac users) and Edge.

### What do I do if something isn't loading?

Try these steps in order:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear your browser cache (Settings > Clear Browsing Data)
3. Try a different browser
4. If it still doesn't work, [contact us](/contact)

### Is my data safe?

Yes. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We don't share your information with anyone. See our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy) for full details.
