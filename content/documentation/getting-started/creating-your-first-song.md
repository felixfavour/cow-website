# Creating Your First Song

Your song library is the heart of Cloud of Worship. Everything starts here — once your songs are in, planning a service takes just minutes.

Let's add your first song together! 🎵

## Before You Start

You'll need:
- Your Cloud of Worship account open
- The song's **title** and **artist** (that's all you really need to get started)
- Optionally: the lyrics, key, and tempo

Don't worry about having everything perfect. You can always edit and add more details later.

## Step 1 — Go to Your Song Library

From the home screen, click **Songs** in the top navigation bar.

You'll see your song library — empty for now, but not for long!

## Step 2 — Click "Add New Song"

Look for the **+ Add New Song** button (usually in the top-right corner of the library page). Click it.

A form will appear asking for your song details.

## Step 3 — Fill in the Basics

You'll see fields for:

- **Song Title** — e.g., "Amazing Grace" *(required)*
- **Artist / Songwriter** — e.g., "John Newton" *(optional but helpful)*
- **Key** — e.g., "G Major" *(don't stress if you're not sure — you can skip this)*
- **Tempo / BPM** — e.g., "72" *(optional)*
- **Tags** — e.g., "hymn, traditional, slow" *(helps with searching later)*

**Fill in the title at minimum**, and you're already good to go.

## Step 4 — Add Your Lyrics

Scroll down to the **Lyrics** section. You can:

- **Type or paste lyrics directly** into the text editor
- **Organize by section** — mark sections like *Verse 1*, *Chorus*, *Bridge*

> **Tip:** If you're not sure about the lyrics yet, you can skip this step and come back later. The song will still be saved.

## Step 5 — Save Your Song

Click the **Save Song** button at the bottom of the form.

That's it! Your song now lives in your library. 🎉

## What You Can Do Next

Now that your first song is saved, here are some handy things to do:

- **Add chords** — click the song and look for a "Chords" tab
- **Attach a recording** — helpful for your team to learn the song
- **Add notes** — things like "We use a capo on the 3rd fret for this one"

When you're ready, check out:
- [Organizing Your Songs](/documentation/guides/organizing-your-songs) — keep your library neat as it grows
- [Planning Your First Service](/documentation/getting-started/your-first-service) — put your song in a setlist
