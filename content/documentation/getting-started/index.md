# Getting Started with Cloud of Worship

In the next 10 minutes, you'll:
- Create your first song
- Learn where everything is
- Understand how your team will use it

Let's go! 🎵

## What is Cloud of Worship?

Cloud of Worship helps you organize your songs, plan services, and collaborate with your team—all in one place. Think of it as your worship planning headquarters.

## Your First Song

:::step-by-step
1. **Click "Create New Song"** from the home screen
   [Screenshot here]

2. **Fill in the song details** — title, artist, key (don't worry if you're not sure about the key!)
   [Screenshot showing form]

3. **Add lyrics or paste them in** — we'll help format them
   [Screenshot showing lyrics editor]

4. **Save and you're done!** Your song is now in your library
   [Screenshot showing saved song]
:::

:::tip
**Pro Tip:** You can add chord notations, arrangements, and even attach audio files later. Start simple and add details as you go.
:::
