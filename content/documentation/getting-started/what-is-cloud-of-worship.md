# What is Cloud of Worship?

Think of Cloud of Worship as your worship team's home base — a place where everything lives together so Sunday mornings feel less stressful and more joyful.

## The Short Answer

Cloud of Worship is a web-based tool that helps worship teams:

- **Organize songs** — keep your whole library in one place, searchable and tidy
- **Plan services** — build a setlist for any service in minutes
- **Collaborate with your team** — everyone sees the same thing, in real time
- **Display on screen** — project lyrics and slides directly from your browser

You don't need to install anything. It works right in your web browser — on your laptop, phone, or tablet.

## Why Worship Teams Love It

Before Cloud of Worship, many teams were juggling:

- 📄 Folders of Word documents with song lyrics
- 📧 Endless email threads about Sunday's setlist
- 💾 USB drives passed around before service
- 🤷 Confusion when someone changed the key last minute

Cloud of Worship brings everything into one place so your team is always on the same page — literally.

## Who Is It For?

Cloud of Worship works for teams of all sizes:

- **Solo worship leaders** who want to stay organized
- **Small church bands** with 2–5 musicians
- **Large worship teams** with multiple services and rotating musicians
- **Tech teams** who run the screens and projectors

You don't need to be tech-savvy to use it. If you can use email, you can use Cloud of Worship.
